## Decisions made

- **`resample_daily` uses trade_date groupby, not session_id.** 3-line pandas implementation. Matches TradeStation/TradingView/CME convention. No session-gap detection needed.
- **RSI avg_loss=0 → RSI=100.** Standard convention for all-gain periods. Uses `.where()` to avoid NaN propagation from division by zero.
- **VWAP uses close × volume accumulation (not typical price).** For futures with OHLCV only, close is the best available proxy for typical price. No HLC average field in the schema.
- **Manifests written for all new session-05 files.** Pre-existing session-04 files (1m, 5m, 15m CLEAN) are left without manifests — backfill is session 06.
- **VWAP session gap threshold = > 60 minutes** (not >= 60). A 60-minute gap is the maintenance halt duration itself; strictly greater preserves intra-halt bar continuity correctly.
