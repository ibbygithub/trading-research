## Decisions made
- Guard threshold is `< 1.0` (not `< 3.0`) because Pearson kurtosis can legitimately be below 3 for platykurtic distributions, but cannot fall below 1 for any real distribution — this catches the Fisher/excess mistake without false-positives
- Fallback for missing kurtosis is `3.0` (Gaussian assumption) rather than `0.0`; the old `0.0` was downstream of the original bug and would have triggered the new guard
