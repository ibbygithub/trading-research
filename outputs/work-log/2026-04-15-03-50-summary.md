## Decisions made

- Engine iterates bar-by-bar (not vectorized) intentionally — clarity and auditability over speed. Vectorization is a session-10+ concern.
- EOD flat uses `rth.close` from instruments.yaml (15:00 ET for ZN). Overnight holds require `eod_flat: false` in the strategy config — opt-out, not opt-in.
- MAE/MFE is tracked from entry fill bar to exit bar inclusive, using raw bar high/low. This is slightly optimistic for intrabar moves but correct for holding-period analysis.
- Slippage deducted separately from P&L in `net_pnl_usd`; the engine also applies it to the fill price so entry/exit prices are realistic fills, not mid prices.
- `format_summary` uses ASCII-only output to avoid CP1252 encoding errors on Windows terminal.
