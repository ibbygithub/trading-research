## Decisions made

- **Additive back-adjustment confirmed correct**: ZN is a fixed-notional rate product; ratio adjustment would distort tick-size arithmetic. Cumulative −27.52 over 16 years is well within the price range (95–135), no distortion.
- **`contract_sequence` sentinel fix**: The last contract in `relevant` (the one active at `end_date`) gets a ContractPeriod with `data_end = end_date`, not `roll_date - 1`. This was wrong in the original design where `relevant[-1]` was purely a boundary marker.
- **Resample drops zero-close buckets**: Not zero-volume (rare but valid), but zero-close (means no bars contributed). This correctly removes maintenance-halt and holiday gaps without needing calendar awareness in the resampler itself.
- **Nullable ints use `min_count=1`**: `pandas.sum()` of all-NaN returns 0 by default; `min_count=1` returns NaN. Critical for preserving signal about periods with no order-flow data.
