## Open Risks Carried Forward

These are unresolved findings requiring investigation in Session 15 (Indicator Census):

1. **HTF aggregation validation** — higher-timeframe resample correctness not fully audited for bar-boundary edge cases (e.g., 15m bars spanning a session boundary, OHLC ordering under thin overnight periods).

2. **Indicator look-ahead strictness under next-bar-open fill** — indicators compute at bar T close; fills execute at bar T+1 open. Any indicator that uses bar T+1 open in its own computation would introduce forward leakage. Surface read found no violations; systematic audit is pending.

3. **Unadjusted ZN roll consumption audit** — strategy and indicator code must consume the back-adjusted series. `rebuild_features()` globs explicitly for `backadjusted` files; full audit of all load paths pending.
